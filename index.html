About

I am a software developer and designer who brings an artistic perspective to my work. My interest in web design began in the early 2000s creating custom myspace themes and building online forums for gaming communities. When I'm not coding, I enjoy 3D modeling in Blender and working within Photoshop. I also have a passion for classic first-person shooter games like DOOM and Quake.


My background in graphic design allows me to approach coding challenges creatively. I'm able to iterate and test different solutions until I find what works best. Through my career, I've used my design skills to make visually appealing, easy-to-use experiences. I enjoy finding new ways to solve problems efficiently and effectively.
